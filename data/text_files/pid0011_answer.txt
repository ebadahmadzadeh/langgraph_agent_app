The 45-year-old female patient with a history of restrictive AN, binge-purge behavior, old traumatic brain injury, and cholecystitis was treated with appropriate antibiotics, underwent an inpatient MRCP, and was referred to surgeons for elective laparoscopic cholecystectomy once deemed fit. She was finally discharged in stable condition to an Eating Disorders Unit for continuation of her treatment for AN. Close monitoring of her weight, liver function tests, and gallbladder status will be carried out, and she will be advised to follow strict COVID-19 protocols to prevent infection. However, further evaluation of her cognitive and memory problems will need to be arranged outside of the given information.
